About Our Group

We are a small group of gardening enthusiasts who like to grow veggies and herbs, fruits and flowers in our front yards.

Greening our streets makes everyone happier. We've been supporting each other for a few years and love to share our hobby with neighbours.

Grow with us!-- We meet every Saturday at 1PM at the Greenopolis Farmers' Market.
